Khvicha Kvaratskhelia !

Georgian professional footballer who plays as a winger for Serie A club "Napoli" and the Georgia national team.

Who is Khvicha kvarastkhelia?

    Over the years there have been many ‘new Maradonas’. Very few have lived up to the huge billing, but one who just might do is Khvicha Kvaratskhelia. The ‘Georgian Maradona’, or ‘Kvaradona as he is known in Naples, has enjoyed a sensational start to his career at the Diego Armando Maradona Stadium. The whole of Italy and not just Napoli fans are currently going wild for Kvaratskhelia. The 21 year old – named Serie A Player of the Month for August has been head-and-shoulders the standout player in Italy so far this season. He has enjoyed an incredible start to his career at Napoli since joining from Dinamo Batumi for just €10 million this summer. He has scored four goals in five games for Napoli thus far.
    The youngster is fearless, always demanding possession and wanting to take responsibility.

Player Of The Month: Khvicha kvarastkhelia!

Khvicha Kvaratskhelia has been named the EA Sports Player Of The Month for August, in the latest plaudits for the Italian club’s new signing. Kvaratskhelia was the most voted-for player by fans in the poll that also included Paulo Dybala of Roma, Teun Koopmeiners of Atalanta, Lautaro Martinez of Inter and Juventus’ Dusan Vlahovic.
The Georgian winger had a great impact on the league right from the start: three goals and an assist in the first four games only tell a part of his performance, made of talent, technique and courage in his plays”, Lega Serie A CEO Luigi De Siervo said in his praise for the midfielder.
